Precipitation Graph



The Precipitation Graph displays the the amount of rain observed by the stations located in various parts of Hawaii. Upon observation of the graph, it will be easy to notice the spikes in inches for precipitation. The higher spikes occur periodically between the months of February and April. Given the findings it is safe to assume the majority of rainfall occurs around the Spring time or the earlier months of the year. Using this data to plan a trip to Hawaii, it would be wise to avoid the earlier months since the chances of rain highly likely.
